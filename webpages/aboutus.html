TravelEx was founded in 2020 by a team of passionate travelers who wanted to revolutionize the travel industry. We started with a vision to create a platform that would make it easier for people to plan, book, and manage their travel experiences. Over the years, we have grown to become a trusted partner for thousands of travelers around the world.
